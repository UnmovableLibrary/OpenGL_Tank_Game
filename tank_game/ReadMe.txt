OpenGL Tank Game

Controls : 

	w - move forward
	a - rotate/turn left
	s - rotate/turn right
	d - move backward
	b - toggle the bird's eye view
	mouse movement - controls where the tank gun is pointing to
	left click - fires a bullet from the tank gun

User Tank : 

	3 hit points
	unlimited ammo

Enemy Tanks :

	3 hit points
	unlimited ammo
